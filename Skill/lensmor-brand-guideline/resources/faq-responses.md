## Response Guidelines

**Tone Checklist for FAQ Responses:**
- [ ] Friendly and approachable
- [ ] Clear and jargon-free (or jargon explained)
- [ ] Honest and transparent
- [ ] Helpful with clear next steps
- [ ] Confident but not arrogant

**Structure Tips:**
- Lead with the direct answer
- Provide context or details second
- End with a call-to-action or link when appropriate
- Use bullet points for scannability
- Keep responses concise (aim for 2-4 sentences for simple questions)

**What to Avoid:**
- Overpromising features or timelines
- Negative comparisons to competitors
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Vague or evasive answers
- Making the user feel bad for asking
