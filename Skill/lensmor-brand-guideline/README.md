Provides comprehensive brand context, visual design rules, writing style guidelines, and product information to ensure brand consistency across all content and communications.

## Overview

This skill teaches Claude about your brand identity:

- **Product positioning** - Core messaging and value proposition
- **Visual design system** - Colors, typography, logo usage
- **Writing style** - Brand voice and content templates
- **Competitive positioning** - Differentiation and market position
- **Standard responses** - Pre-approved answers to common questions

## When to Use

Use this skill when:
- Creating marketing materials or product documentation
- Writing announcements or user communications
- Designing visual assets or UI components
- Responding to customer inquiries
- Developing any brand-consistent content

## How to Use

Activate the skill by referencing it:
- "Using the brand guidelines skill, create a feature announcement..."
- "Help me write a product description following brand guidelines"

The skill automatically:
- Applies correct brand voice and tone
- Uses proper terminology and product naming
- Follows visual design specifications
- Maintains brand consistency

## Best Practices

- **Be Specific**: Provide concrete examples, not vague guidelines
- **Show Examples**: Include good and bad examples
- **Keep Updated**: Review quarterly and after major releases
- **Include Context**: Explain why, not just what
- **Make Practical**: Focus on day-to-day usage

## Maintenance

- Review guidelines quarterly
- Update after product releases
- Refresh competitor information
- Add new FAQ responses as needed
