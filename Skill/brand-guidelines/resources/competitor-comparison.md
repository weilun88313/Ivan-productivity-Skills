## Messaging Guidelines for Comparisons

**Do:**
- Focus on our unique strengths
- Use factual, verifiable claims
- Highlight customer success stories
- Compare features objectively
- Lead with customer benefits

**Don't:**
- Disparage competitors by name (unless specifically asked)
- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Compare on features we don't have
- Get defensive
- Ignore legitimate competitor strengths
