## Writing Principles Demonstrated

### Across All Examples

**Hook Techniques**:
- Numbers ("3 years ago...")
- Questions ("Would you rather...")
- Controversy ("Unpopular opinion...")
- Promises ("Here's how to...")
- Specificity ("$50K from one tweet")

**Body Principles**:
- One idea per tweet in threads
- Whitespace for readability
- Bullets/lists for scannability
- Mix of insight + example

**CTA Patterns**:
- Reply prompts ("What's yours?")
- Save prompts ("Save this")
- Action prompts ("Try this")
- Opinion prompts ("Agree?")

## Template Extraction

### Universal Thread Template
```
1/ [Hook: Bold claim or question]

[Context: Why it matters]

2/ [Problem: What most people get wrong]

[Consequence: Why it matters]

3-5/ [Solution: Your framework/insights]

[For each: Principle + Example/Explanation]

N/ [Conclusion: Summary + Call to action]
```

### Universal Single-Tweet Template
```
[Hook line]

[Insight or observation]

[Memorable conclusion or question]
```
